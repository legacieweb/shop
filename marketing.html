10‑Day Rapid Acquisition Plan (fastest routes to clients)

Goal: leads now, revenue in < 10 days

Day 0–1: Prep & Positioning

  • Craft one‑liner: “Launch a high‑converting shop in minutes. Payments, delivery, analytics—all in one.”
  • Spin up demo store (seed with 6–10 products, images, variants, coupons) and public demo URL.
  • Record 90‑second Loom walkthrough (store creation ➜ add product ➜ checkout ➜ dashboard analytics).
  • Write 3 short case outlines (e.g., IG seller, local boutique, digital course).
  • Landing improvements: clear CTA, social proof placeholders, FAQ, pricing with 4‑day rapid trial.
Offer: “Try ShopRight free for 4 days. Setup help included. Launch your store today.”

Day 1–2: Prospecting & Direct Outreach

  • Build 300‑500 lead list: IG hashtags (#shopsale, #smallbusiness), LinkedIn (owner + ecommerce), Facebook groups, local directories.
  • Segment by use‑case (apparel, cosmetics, electronics, artisans, services).
  • Start multi‑channel outreach: Email + LinkedIn + IG/WhatsApp DM. 80–120 touches/day.
  • Book 15‑30 min demo slots on Calendly/Google Form link.

Day 2–3: Social Launch + Community

  • Launch thread on LinkedIn & X (Twitter): problem ➜ demo ➜ early adopter offer ➜ CTA.
  • Post in relevant communities with value (setup checklists, free store review offer).
  • Short video reels: “Create a store in 60 seconds” + “Add Pay on Delivery”.
  • Pin launch posts, reply fast, move interested users to DM → demo.

Day 3–4: Listings & Directories

  • Product Hunt upcoming page + teaser, IndieHackers launch post.
  • AlternativeTo, BetaList, Makerlog, StartupBase, SideProjectors, SaasHub listings.
  • Developer angle: GitHub repo + README + demo links for embeddable widget concept.

Day 4–5: Partnerships & Agencies

  • Pitch small marketing/dev agencies: white‑label storefronts for their clients.
  • Payment gateway co‑marketing angle (Paystack, Flutterwave) featuring ShopRight.
  • Startup hubs/co‑working spaces: free workshop “Launch a shop in 30 minutes”.

Day 5–7: Referral & Onboarding Sprint

  • Introduce referral: “Invite 3 sellers → get 2 extra months free.”
  • Run onboarding sessions (daily live demo Q&A). Record + share replay.
  • Concierge setup for first 20 users (import products, theme set, payment config).

Day 7–8: Content That Converts

  • Publish 5 quick guides: WhatsApp → Storefront, IG Bio Store, Coupons that convert, COD vs Gateway, First sale playbook.
  • Country landing pages: “Create an online store in Nigeria/Kenya/UK”.
  • Feature blog: “From spreadsheet to store in 10 minutes”.

Day 8–10: Push Demos → Paying

  • Follow‑up cadence: D+1, D+3, D+7 with value (template below).
  • Offer migration help from WhatsApp/IG spreadsheets.
  • Publish 2 early testimonials; use in all outbound.
