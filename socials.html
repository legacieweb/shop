Profile Optimization (Day 0 Setup)

Bio & Positioning

  • One‑liner: “Turn your DMs into orders. Launch a store in minutes.”
  • Social proof: “Trusted by IG/WhatsApp sellers and SMEs.”
  • CTA: “Start free setup today → {link}”

Link‑in‑bio structure

  • 1. Start free setup (primary CTA)
  • 2. Book a demo
  • 3. Demo store
  • 4. Community group

Add UTM tags per platform for tracking.

Visuals & Highlights

  • Pin 3 posts: product demo, testimonial, quickstart
  • Story highlights: Setup, Payments, Coupons, Wins
  • Cover images: simple bold benefits + CTA

20‑Day No‑Ads Social Plan (Daily Actions)

Days 1–3: Foundations + Soft Launch

  • Optimize profiles + link‑in‑bio with UTM.
  • Publish: 1 reel/TikTok (60s store setup) + 1 carousel (benefits) + 1 thread (build‑in‑public).
  • Warm network: DM 50 contacts (friends, clients, alumni) for feedback + intros.
  • Join 5–10 FB/LinkedIn groups; post value checklist teaser (no links; comments for opt‑in).
Offer: “Free concierge setup for first 20 sellers.”

Days 4–7: Outreach + Lives

  • Direct outreach 60–100/day across IG/LinkedIn/Twitter (scripts below).
  • IG Live/Twitter Space: “Store makeover in 15 minutes.” Pin replay.
  • Collab with 2 micro‑creators (rev‑share; no upfront).
  • Daily comments: 30 min on niche creators; add value, move to DMs.

Days 8–12: Proof & Education

  • Publish 3 short case snippets (before/after, checkout speed, coupon usage).
  • How‑to series: COD vs gateway, coupons that convert, WhatsApp → store.
  • Community post: free group for sellers; weekly office hours.
  • Run “Launch Challenge”: we set up your shop this week, share your link.

Days 13–16: Collabs + UGC

  • Co‑post with partners (payment providers, agencies) featuring quick guides.
  • Prompt new users to post a launch announcement (template provided) and tag us.
  • Repost UGC; add carousel of best shops created this week.
  • Host AMA Q&A; capture objections for FAQ content.

Days 17–20: Conversion Sprint

  • Follow‑ups: D+1, D+3, D+7 sequences for all leads (scripts below).
  • Publish 2 testimonial graphics + 1 walkthrough reel.
  • Referral push: “Invite 3 sellers → get 2 months free.”
  • Book final demos via polls and stories with countdown stickers.

Posting cadence

  • Instagram/TikTok: 1 short video daily + 3–5 stories.
  • LinkedIn: 1 post/day or 5 posts/week.
  • Twitter/X: 1 thread + 2–3 tweets/day.
  • FB Groups: 1–2 value posts/week + daily comments.

Platform‑Specific Strategies (Organic Only)

Instagram

  • Reels: 30–60s “setup in minutes”, “coupon in 20s”, “COD on/off”. Add captions & CTA overlay.
  • Carousels: pain → solution → steps → CTA; feature checklists.
  • Stories: polls, Q&A, countdowns to lives; link stickers to demo/trial.
  • Collabs: co‑author posts with creators/partners to share audiences.
  • Hashtags: niche+intent (see below). Pin top 3 posts.

TikTok

  • Hook in first 3 seconds; demonstrate setup and checkout quickly.
  • Green screen react to sellers’ pain points; show fix in ShopRight.
  • SEO captions: “how to create an online store fast”, “sell on WhatsApp with checkout”.
  • Pin 3 top videos; go Live for Q&A 2×/week.

LinkedIn

  • Case posts: “Before/After: seller launched in 1 day → first 10 orders.”
  • Carousel docs: step��by‑step “store in 30 minutes”.
  • Outbound to SME owners/agency founders; offer free setup.
  • Comment thoughtfully on SMB/e‑commerce leaders; move to DM.

Twitter/X

  • Threads: build‑in‑public, checklists, quick wins for sellers.
  • Reply to relevant hashtags (#smallbusiness, #ecommerce, #indiebiz).
  • Host Spaces: “Audit your store live”.

Facebook Groups

  • Value posts (no links): offer checklist/audit; DM those who comment.
  • Answer questions daily; invite to free setup via DM.
  • Respect group rules; provide proofs and examples.

WhatsApp/Telegram

  • Start a broadcast list and a seller community group.
  • Share quick wins, templates, and launch announcements.
  • Weekly office hours link; encourage referrals.

Objection handling (reply snippets)

“I’m busy.”

No problem — send 5 product photos + prices and we’ll build it today. You get a link to share in your bio and stories.

“Customers prefer chatting first.”

Keep DMs for questions. The shop gives a trusted checkout for ready buyers and fewer lost orders.

“I’m not technical.”

It’s no‑code. We do the setup, you just share your link. We can also enable Pay on Delivery to start.

“Payments are hard.”

We support COD + gateways like Paystack/Flutterwave. Setup takes minutes.
